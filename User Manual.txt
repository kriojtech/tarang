User Manual
Welcome

Thank you for choosing Tarang Smart RF Finder.

Tarang is a wireless item-finding device designed to help you quickly locate important belongings such as keys, wallets, bags, remote controls, or other personal items. With a simple press of a button on the transmitter, the paired receiver emits a loud beep and flashes an LED, making it easy to find misplaced items.

Charging the Receiver
Connect the receiver to a USB Type-C charger.
The charging indicator turns Red while charging.
The indicator turns Blue when fully charged.
Disconnect the charger after charging is complete.
First-Time Use
Fully charge the receiver.
Switch ON the receiver.
Keep the transmitter within operating range.
Press the corresponding button on the transmitter.
The receiver will flash and beep.
Normal Operation
To Locate an Item
Press the assigned button on the transmitter.
The paired receiver starts:
Flashing LED
Loud buzzer
Follow the sound to locate your item.
Press the receiver button (if provided) or wait for the alarm to stop automatically.
Battery Status

A low battery may result in:

Reduced operating range
Lower buzzer volume
Dim LED brightness

Recharge the receiver when these symptoms appear.

Charging Tips
Use a standard 5 V USB charger.
Avoid overcharging for extended periods.
Recharge before long-term storage.
Safety Instructions
Do not immerse in water.
Do not expose to excessive heat.
Keep away from children under three years.
Do not attempt to open the battery.
Use only a suitable USB charger.
Troubleshooting
Receiver does not beep

Possible causes:

Battery discharged
Receiver switched OFF
Out of operating range

Solution:

Recharge the receiver.
Switch it ON.
Move closer to the transmitter.
LED does not blink

Possible causes:

Battery low
Receiver OFF

Solution:

Recharge and restart the receiver.
Short operating range

Possible causes:

Low battery
Metal obstacles
Strong RF interference

Solution:

Recharge the battery.
Reduce obstructions.
Move away from interference sources.
Care and Maintenance
Keep the device clean and dry.
Store in a cool place.
Recharge every few months if not used regularly.
